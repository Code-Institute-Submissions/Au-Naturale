Services

Wash n Go £30

We'll shampoo your hair to rid it of all the the dirt, then condition. Afterwards we'll detangle your hair using a detangler bursh.Then we'll saturate it with our natural hair products and then apply our styling gel. Then finally dry your hair slowly to get achieve that Wash n Go effect.

Deep Condition £40

We'll use a gentle shampoo to first wash your hair. Then apply a deep condtioning products from our natural hair collection to your shampooed hair. Then we'll place you under one of our heat Caps for 20 minutes then rinse. We'll use the LOC (liquid,oil,cream) method to saturate the hair then dry.

Clay Mask Treatment £55

The Clay Mask treamtent is very beneficial your hair, Bentonite clay is antimicrobial and helps irritated or flaking scalp. Bentonite clay contains a mixture of natural minerals that are beneficial for your hair and can encourage hair growth. It moisturizes, conditions, softens, and reduces frizz. Firstly, shampoo, your hair and blow dry. We'll section your you hair to apply the Bentonite Clay. we'll leave it on for 10-15 minutes (dependant on clients scalp sensitivity). Then moisturise the hair and blow dry.

Twists out £60

You might desire a new look for your natural hair. With twist outs you can manipulate the texture of your hair. we'll shampoo and condition your hair then moisturise. We'll then proceed to twist your hair whilst applying the suitable styling products to ensure your twists lasts as long as possible. Then place you under our heater for an 1 hour then remove the twists for for the twist out effect.

Hair Trim £45

Whilst your on your natural journey, dead ends may affect the growth off your hair. We offer Hair trim to trim those dead ends. We can trim your hair wet or dry for cutting the dead ends, depending on your preference. We also provide hair trims if you'd prefere a different style for you natural hair, such as layers or a different length.

The Big Chop £35

We can give the boost you need to start your natural hair journey! The big chop is cutting all your hai off to start over,We offer the to do the big chop for your so you don't chicken out. We'll also offer any advice Afterwards on haircare and treamtents.
